Outdoor Activities

Hiking in the Japanese Alps

Explore the breathtaking trails of the Japanese Alps and experience the beauty of untouched nature.

Skiing in Hokkaido

Enjoy world-class skiing and snowboarding on the powdery slopes of Hokkaido, Japan’s winter paradise.

Scuba Diving in Okinawa

Dive into the crystal-clear waters of Okinawa to discover vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life.

Mount Fuji Climbing

Climb Japan's most iconic peak, Mount Fuji, and take in the magnificent panoramic views.

Surfing in Chiba

Hit the waves on the beaches of Chiba, a hotspot for surfers from all around the world.

Kayaking in Lake Biwa

Paddle through Japan’s largest freshwater lake, surrounded by scenic mountains and wildlife.

Paragliding in Nagano

Soar above the beautiful landscapes of Nagano with an unforgettable paragliding experience.

Cycling the Shimanami Kaido

Cycle along the scenic Shimanami Kaido, connecting Japan’s islands with breathtaking sea views.

Heritage Attractions

Kyoto’s Ancient Temples

Step back in time as you explore Kyoto’s magnificent temples and shrines, preserving Japan’s rich history.

Himeji Castle

Visit the spectacular Himeji Castle, one of Japan’s most iconic and well-preserved feudal castles.

Itsukushima Shrine, Miyajima

Admire the iconic floating torii gate at Itsukushima Shrine, a UNESCO World Heritage site on Miyajima Island.

Nara’s Todaiji Temple

Discover the world’s largest bronze Buddha statue inside the historic Todaiji Temple in Nara.

Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)

Marvel at the shimmering beauty of the Golden Pavilion in Kyoto, surrounded by tranquil gardens.

Osaka Castle

Explore the rich history of Osaka Castle, a symbol of Japan’s samurai past and a key historical site.

Local Experiences

Tea Ceremonies in Kyoto

Participate in a traditional tea ceremony and experience Japan’s refined cultural practices.

Sumo Wrestling in Tokyo

Watch a live sumo wrestling match, one of Japan’s most ancient and revered sports.

Kimono Fitting in Asakusa

Wear a traditional kimono and stroll through the streets of Asakusa, experiencing Japan’s cultural heritage firsthand.

Soba Making in Nagano

Learn the art of soba noodle making in Nagano, a hands-on culinary experience not to be missed.

Sake Breweries in Kyoto

Tour sake breweries in regions like Niigata or Hyogo to learn about the sake-making process and sample different varieties.

Kabuki Performance in Fukuoka

Attend traditional Japanese theater performances to appreciate the unique storytelling, music, and elaborate costumes.

Relaxation & Wellness

Onsen Hot Springs

Relax in one of Japan’s natural hot springs (onsen) and experience ultimate rejuvenation.

Zen Meditation Retreats

Find peace and tranquility in a traditional Zen meditation retreat in Japan.

Aromatherapy in Tokyo

Indulge in a luxurious aromatherapy session in Tokyo, designed to relax both body and mind.

Shiatsu Massage in Kyoto

Experience a traditional shiatsu massage in Kyoto, targeting pressure points for deep relaxation.

Forest Bathing in Aomori

Immerse yourself in nature with a calming forest bathing experience in the lush forests of Aomori.

Reiki Healing in Tokyo

Restore your energy and balance through a traditional Reiki healing session in Tokyo.

Family-Friendly Activities

Tokyo Disneyland

Enjoy a magical family experience at Tokyo Disneyland, packed with fun rides and attractions for all ages.

LEGOLAND Japan Resort

Visit LEGOLAND Japan Resort for an exciting and interactive family adventure.

Ueno Zoo, Tokyo

Take the kids to Ueno Zoo, home to pandas, lions, and many more animals that will captivate children and adults alike.

Fuji-Q Highland

Enjoy thrilling roller coasters and rides at Fuji-Q Highland, an amusement park located near Mount Fuji.

Aquarium in Okinawa

Explore Okinawa’s Churaumi Aquarium, home to whale sharks, manta rays, and vibrant marine life.

Universal Studios Japan

Experience the magic of movies and thrilling rides at Universal Studios Japan, perfect for families.
